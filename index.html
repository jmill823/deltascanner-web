How it works

01

We source the signal

Financial pressure from county records. Regulatory enforcement from municipal systems. Behavioral indicators from public filings. Multiple independent data streams that most platforms can't access — and none of them combine.

02

We join and score

Every parcel gets a composite distress score (0–100) calibrated to its market. The model uses the best signal each city publishes. No two cities score the same way — because no two cities publish the same data.

03

The list moves

Source data updates daily. Enforcement escalates. Payments get missed. ~15% of parcels near the threshold shift every month. Month 2 is a different list than month 1.
